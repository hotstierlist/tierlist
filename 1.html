Please note: This player list is built on years of accumulated data (dating back to 2019), including lifetime statistics sourced directly from HeroesProfile, along with extensive research and analysis of individual player behavior. Every effort has been made to ensure objectivity and impartiality. Core values used in this list—such as Skill, KDA, Kill Participation, Talent Evaluation, and Player Win Rate—are AI-generated, with additional AI assistance used for evaluating builds and play patterns. Important: This player list does not rank players purely by mechanical skill. Instead, it answers the question: "Which player is the best potential teammate?" For example, a player might have exceptional skill (S tier), but if they are also toxic, disruptive, or abusive (F tier), those traits are factored in and may bring their overall placement down to an average rank like C tier. This list is intended as an impartial, fact-based resource to help the community identify the most reliable and promising teammates. If you disagree with a player’s placement, you’re ultimately disagreeing with your own publicly available performance data and demonstrated behavior.

HOW IT WORKS:

Signature Heroes Directly fed from HeroesProfile data. If a player has more than 1 account on HeroesProfile (smurfs), they're averaged out between them. There is no past cutoff.
Skill Rating. Directly fed from HeroesProfile's available data. Solo & Duo 100% Strength. Trio 50% Strength. Quad 20% Strength. 5-Stack 5% Strength. While the secret sauce (formula) for calculating Skill won't be revealed to prevent abuse, let's just say that neither winrate nor number of games matter.
KDA & KDR. Directly fed from HeroesProfile's available data. KDA on display is primarily for contrast to the Skill and it is not vital to the formula, there's a lot more going on. Players who primarily play sololaner will get automatic KDA boost (it's complicated, but short version would be HPScore difference between them and their teammates), because of the nature of their role, to equivalent with everyone else's. The ☠ icon is placed on individuals of D or F KDA. Solo & Duo 100% Strength. Trio 75% Strength. Quad 50% Strength. 5-Stack 25% Strength. Abathur Influence only 25% Strength (to prevent frequent Abathur players skyrocketing, essentially cheating the KDA). HPScore deviation from bigger to smaller stacks is punished. KDA requirement raises as Skill goes up (Generally a sign of skilled players trolling and playing like pepegas).
Average Kill Participation (aka how much you actually help your team secure kills). Passively tied to Skill, as Kill Participation requirement goes up the higher the Skill rating. QM, Unranked and Ranked only.
Individual Win Rate Percentage. Solo Queue represents the vast majority of the stats. Duo Queue (and in some rare case Trio queue) win rate is used slightly as a comparative measure. A win rate above 60.89% is considered S-tier. Protection against early level abuse for smurf accounts is in place.
Positioning Evaluation. Tied to and subset of "Skill".
Talent Choices Evaluation correlates closely with win rate, thereby favoring and punishing outliers at both ends of the spectrum. Penalizes players who experiment with unconventional builds, unless they keep a consistent positive track record as well as those who fail to consistently adhere to established strategies. Anchored to Skill & Winrate (cannot go further than 2 tiers).

User Input. Takes into account both the enjoyment and the quality of the games played, as well as the mutual respect, support, and group member growth. Additionally, it is considered how well the individuals treat their fellow party members and the overall positive atmosphere and experience that creates lasting, pleasant memories of the time spent together. Social equates to how available a player is - whether or not they are public, easy to reach, streaming, responsive to inquiries, can they often be found in voicechat with others, etc.
Different Gamemode Differentiation Currently all stats from Ranked, QM, UD, ARAM are used together with equal strength. If such a need occurs in the future, they could be separated into several tierlists, one for each gamemode. Mirror matches excluded for ARAM only
Alt. IDs Disclamer The Alternate IDs serve the sole purpose of indicating which accounts' statistics are considered for Skill/KDA calculation, rather than revealing smurfs. Identifying player smurfs is not the intention, and it's impossible to know every smurf account out there.

  • Very inactive players will be removed after awhile (They might still appear in the Subjective List)
  • Additional correction for very old games on HeroesProfile that do not show stack-size correctly
